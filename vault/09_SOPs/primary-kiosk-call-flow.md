# Primary Kiosk Call Flow

## Call Flow Script

**Step 1 — Greet and ask for address**
> "Hi, good morning! This is ____ and I'm in training. What is the address you are heading to?"

**Step 2 — Ask visiting or company**
> "Are you Visiting or from a company?"
- If with a company: ask for the spelling of their company name

**Step 3 — Request physical ID**
> "Please place your ID horizontally facing up inside the tray."
- Take note of the first and last name from the ID

**Step 4 — Ask for resident name**
> "What is the name of the Resident?"

**Step 5 — Call the resident (if needed)**
> "One moment, I will call the resident for your access."
→ Proceed to call the resident (see primary-calling-resident-for-access)
→ Add tile if needed, add access, send visitor confirmation, then go back to the visitor

**Step 6 — Save tag**
> "WE WILL BE SAVING YOUR TAG FOR EASY FUTURE ACCESS, IS THIS YOUR CAR?"
→ Add tag if needed
→ Always check the community post order for tag-saving rules

**Step 7 — Release visitor**
> "You can take your ID and move forward. We will open the gate once you get there."
→ Perform LOG ENTRY: enter name of driver if needed, click recent LPR, verify tag if applicable
→ Open Gate — Visitor Gate

## Entry Exceptions (Default)

The following categories do not require a physical ID check by default:
- **Pre-approved marked vehicles** (e.g., hotwire, Amazon, UPS, USPS) — verify against the HOA ADDRESS pre-approved list
- **Residents in the car** — resident can state security questions, show their own ID, or call their number
- **Emergency vehicles** — grant immediate entry
- **Registered license plate or RFID tag** — if the tag is registered in the system under the driver's name (guest or resident), no additional ID is required

Always check the community's post orders and announcements for community-specific overrides to these defaults.
