# Heritage Palms South Post Order - Outside Work Repair Vendors Such As

## Rule

Outside work or repair vendors, such as roofers, contractors, and maintenance, only allowed M-F 6am-6pm and Sat 9am-3:30pm. No vendors allowed on Sun. Vendors: Plumbing, Utility, Electric, A/C, Special deliveries are allowed anytime.
