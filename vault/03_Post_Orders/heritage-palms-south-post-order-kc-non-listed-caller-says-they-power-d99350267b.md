# Heritage Palms South Post Order - Non Listed Caller Says They Power

## Rule

Non-listed caller says they are the 'Power of Attorney' (POA). We submit a 'POA Confirmation' ticket requesting they be added as a POA Resident. As we await confirmation, the person can add a guest for up to 3 days.
